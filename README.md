## 🧠 About

I design and ship end-to-end products at the intersection of **AI**, **developer tooling**, and **compliance / security**. I care about the boring middle 90% of software — the parts that decide whether a demo becomes a product: auth, multi-tenancy, evaluation, observability, and the unglamorous workflow loops that real users actually live inside.

- 🚀 Currently building **[Complico](https://github.com/Hanishsaini/complico)** — an AI Compliance Copilot for SOC 2 & ISO 27001.
- 🛠️ Comfortable across the stack: Python / FastAPI on the back, Next.js / React / TypeScript on the front, with FAISS, sentence-transformers, and Llama in the middle.
- 📚 Always reading about LLM evals, RAG, and how to make AI systems *trustworthy enough to ship*.
- 💬 Open to talking about: AI product design, compliance automation, GTM for technical founders, or anything that sits between a model and a real user.

## ✍️ A few things I believe

- **Ship the boring middle.** Auth, evals, observability, and onboarding are the product.
- **Make the AI part falsifiable.** If you can't measure it, you can't sell it.
- **Compliance is a workflow problem, not a checklist problem.** Build the loop, not the form.
- **Small teams + sharp wedges beat big teams + broad roadmaps.**
